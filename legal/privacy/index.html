privacy

last updated march 14, 2026

short version
we collect the minimum practical information needed to run the site: email addresses for newsletter and summary unlocks, payment details via stripe, analytics via plausible, and local browser storage for gating state.

what we collect

  • email addresses you submit through signup or unlock forms.
  • billing and subscription information processed by stripe.
  • basic analytics events and pageview information collected by plausible.
  • local browser storage used to remember free or paid access on your device.

how we use it

we use your information to deliver newsletters, unlock gated content, process payments, verify subscriptions, prevent abuse, improve product performance, and understand which pages and workflows are useful.

third-party services

  • stripe handles checkout, recurring billing, payment methods, and customer portal access.
  • beehiiv stores newsletter subscriptions and powers email delivery.
  • plausible measures traffic and site usage in a privacy-conscious analytics environment.

cookies and local storage

xvary uses browser storage to remember whether you unlocked the free tier or verified a paid plan on a given device. this keeps the site usable without a full account system. if you clear browser storage, you may need to re-enter your email or re-verify access.

we do not sell your data

xvary does not sell personal information. we do not broker subscriber lists or package user data for third-party advertising marketplaces.

retention

we retain information only as long as needed for business, operational, legal, or security reasons. newsletter subscriptions remain active until you unsubscribe. billing records are retained through stripe according to stripe's policies and applicable law.

ccpa and gdpr style rights

depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, or restriction of your personal information. we will review reasonable requests in good faith and comply where required by law.

contact

for privacy questions, unsubscribe issues, or data requests, contact hello@xvary.com. if your request involves billing, include the email address used during stripe checkout so we can locate the relevant account information faster.
