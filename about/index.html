about xvary

xvary is an independent equity research platform. every report starts from raw filings, earnings calls, and market data — not from someone else's summary. the system writes in plain english, publishes composite scores with explicit kill criteria, and timestamps every call.

the project exists because institutional-depth research shouldn't cost $25,000 a seat. wall street research is written for compliance, not for the person actually making the trade. xvary writes for the person making the trade.

there's no editorial board, no ad revenue, no sponsored coverage, and no reason to say a stock is good when it isn't. the composite score moves when evidence changes. the kill criteria fire when they fire.

principles

  • show the evidence, protect the recipewe publish the data points, the sources, and the outputs. we don't publish the internal weights, prompt structure, or decision heuristic.
  • composite is not a recommendationthe composite score measures how strong the setup looks after evidence review. it's not a buy signal. every score ships with a thesis, a kill framework, and a variant view.
  • timestamps over reputationsevery call is timestamped. every score change is logged. you can audit the track record, not just the marketing.
  • no conflictsno sponsored coverage, no payment for placement, no ads. revenue comes from subscriptions.

how reports are made

the research pipeline processes sec filings, earnings call transcripts, competitive positioning data, and valuation inputs through a multi-stage system. evidence is routed into structured modules (thesis, valuation, catalysts, risk, financials, competition, operations). each module runs independently. the composite score is assigned only after the full evidence set is reviewed.

humans verify, rewrite, and decide what gets published. the system generates the depth. the editor decides the direction.
