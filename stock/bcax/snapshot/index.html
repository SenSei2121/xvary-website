What they do
Bicara is a biotech betting one antibody drug can help tumors let treatment in.
Ficerafusp alfa stacks two targets: EGFR means the tumor hook, so the drug can stick, and TGF-beta means the scar signal, so tumors lose a shield. That matters in head and neck squamous cell carcinoma, where Bicara says unmet need remains. You are not buying sales here. You are buying 55 employees and one scientific shot.
