What they do
Chain Bridge runs a niche bank and trust business for businesses, nonprofits, political groups, and wealthy families.
This bank wins by being the place certain clients trust with very large balances. It had $1.57 billion of deposits and just 84 employees at December 31, 2025, which means each employee supported about $18.7 million of deposits. If your bank relationship depends on service and discretion, moving it is painful.

What could go wrong

the #1 risk is political deposit runoff — this bank's funding base is a feature until it becomes a flight risk.

!
high
Political deposit concentration
management has exposure to politically linked deposits, and those balances are not the same as a sleepy retail checking base. money tied to election cycles or political activity can move fast.
if deposits leave, the same line that drives 84% of revenue — net interest income — gets pressured first.

med
Small-bank earnings concentration
CBNA generated $55.48M of trailing revenue, with $46.5M from net interest income and only $9.0M from non-interest income. that is a narrow earnings mix.
there is not much fee income to cushion the blow if loan yields compress or deposit costs rise.
