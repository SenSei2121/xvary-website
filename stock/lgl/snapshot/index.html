What could go wrong

the top risk is interest-income dependence — LGL's earnings power is tied far more to cash yields and investments than to selling electronic components.

Interest-income dependence
Last quarter, $2.07M of income came from interest and investments. Products contributed $155K. That means 93% of the current earnings mix sits outside the core operating business.
If rates move lower or portfolio income weakens, most of today's income base comes under pressure at once.
