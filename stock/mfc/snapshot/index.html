  • manulife financial reported favorable operating results for the third quarter.
  • revenues and core earnings per share both improved nicely on a year-to-year basis.
    the asia, global wealth and asset management (wam), and canada operations all fared relatively well during the term. these businesses achieved solid bottom-line gains, though the global wam operation did experience net outflows of c$6.2 billion, driven by several large plan sales in the united states, greater net member withdrawals, and cost of living pressures in north america. The company has agreed to sell mvi life in vietnam. this operation is a separate life insurance entity from the company's core operations in the country, manulife vietnam, which it will continue to operate.

  • we anticipate good operating performance here from 2026 onward.
  • the company appears to be well positioned in the insurance markets that it serves.
    manulife is diversified by business and across geographies, and should be able to capitalize on growth opportunities across its asia and global wam businesses.
