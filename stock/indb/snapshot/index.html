What they do
Independent Bank runs Rockland Trust, which takes your deposits, makes loans, and manages money across Massachusetts and nearby states.
Banking moat (sticky funding → cheap money to lend → better profits) starts with relationships, not apps. INDB has $20.3 billion of deposits, which is more than 5 times its roughly $4 billion market cap, and 1837 employees keeping customers tied to branches, loans, and advice. If your checking account, mortgage, and wealth manager all sit inside Rockland Trust, leaving is paperwork.
