What they do
BancFirst is an Oklahoma bank that takes deposits, makes loans, and runs trust services across 106 locations.

the top risk here is single-state Oklahoma concentration.

single-state Oklahoma concentration
all 106 branches operate in Oklahoma. That means a local slowdown can pressure loan demand, credit quality, and deposit growth at the same time. There is no geographic diversification to bail you out.
