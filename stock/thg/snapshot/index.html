  • hanover insurance has reported good top-line results.
    net premiums earned have increased, thanks to renewal price hikes, stable retention, and a spike in new business. net investment income, a separate revenue line item, has risen due to the impact of higher earned yields on hanover’s fixed maturity investment portfolio. growth in the company’s asset base, an advance in partnership income, and successful portfolio repositioning have also been a plus. management has announced the launch of a new product, hanover specialty industrial property (hsip) advantage. this offering is designed to modernize insurance coverage for small to mid-sized businesses that manufacture, blend, distribute, warehouse, or transport high-hazard products and materials. hsip advantage offers a specialized, modular solution that can be customized with new insurance coverages and endorsements to address emerging risks. the offering includes clear policy language and a straightforward product design that simplifies insurance protection for property risks that are on the edge of insurability in the standard market.
