    the company powered on its first type inspection authorization aircraft late last year, entering the final stage of faa certification after a decade of regulatory work. five tia aircraft are now in production simultaneously, and the company produced 15 times more faa-conforming parts in 2025 than in all of 2024, evidence that manufacturing is keeping pace with the certification.
