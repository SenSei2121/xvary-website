What they do
Northern Trust manages money, safeguards institutional assets, and handles banking for wealthy families, companies, and large investors.
This business wins because moving your custody bank is painful. Asset servicing (holding and administering client assets → the back-office plumbing → clients rarely switch fast) and wealth management relationships tend to stick, and Northern Trust runs that model across 24 U.S. states and 22 locations abroad with 23,300 employees. You are not buying a rocket ship. You are buying a trust business that turned $13.0 billion of equity into a 13% return.

the number that mattered
The 15.45% EPS beat mattered most because it shows the business entered 2026 with better profit momentum than analysts expected.
  • northern trust was carrying some momentum heading into 2026.
    the chicago-based money manager surpassed consensus earnings expectations in each of the last three quarters.
  • strength in net interest income has accounted for much of the recent upside, with results benefiting from higher rates, balance sheet growth, and lower funding costs.
    gains in its trust & investment services business have also been supportive of bottom-line expansion, fueled by rising assets under management (aum) and custody balances from favorable equity markets, particularly in the technology sector.
  • in our view, northern likely closed out 2025 with another solid showing.
  • fourth-quarter results are scheduled to be released on january 22nd.
  • our presentation calls for earnings to reach $9.30 a share this year.
    northern appears to be in a good position to grow its bottom line in 2026, under the assumption of continued global economic resilience. the firm’s wealth management and asset servicing arms were exhibiting solid momentum to close out 2025, and at this juncture, we see no obvious reason why these favorable trends wouldn’t carry over into 2026. higher modeled net interest income, coupled with ai-related productivity gains and a continued focus on expense discipline, also represent key catalysts in northern’s 2026 growth story.
