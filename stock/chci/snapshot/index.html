What they do
Comstock manages, develops, and operates mixed-use real estate around Washington, D.C. for property owners.
Its edge is a long-term asset management agreement covering the entire Anchor Portfolio. Plain English: a related party hired Comstock to run the whole platform, and the contract uses cost-plus pricing (expenses get reimbursed, plus a fee). So what: with just 206 employees and a 20.6% operating margin, you are betting on a fee stream that is hard to dislodge quickly.
