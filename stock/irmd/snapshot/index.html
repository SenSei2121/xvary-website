What they do
Iradimed sells MRI-safe pumps and monitors that keep IVs and vital signs working inside scanner rooms.
MRI rooms punish ordinary gear. IRMD sells hardware built for 30,000 gauss, so your pump does not become a paperweight near the magnet. The 77.4% gross margin says hospitals pay for that safety, and the gap versus generic equipment is the whole business.
