What they do
argenx sells immune-disease medicines, led by VYVGART, a treatment that removes harmful antibodies driving autoimmune attacks.
VYVGART was first to market in FcRn blocking (FcRn blocking → clearing harmful antibodies faster → fewer autoimmune attacks). If your doctor already knows the drug and now has a pre-filled syringe option, switching gets harder. That helps explain why revenue jumped 90% in 2025 while operating margin hit 50.0%.

the number that mattered
$4.2B mattered most because this is still a one-franchise story, and that sales base funds everything else you are paying for.
  • argenx se posted spectacular preliminary results for 2025.
    fully-audited results are expected to be released by the end of february, but the company announced net sales of $4.15 billion.
  • this represented a 90% surge over the previous year's top line.
    the company's sole drug, vyvgart, is highly sought by those suffering from a rare neuromuscular disease, myasthenia gravis. we tentatively estimate that earnings momentum will continue through 2027. we look for earnings to come in at roughly $28.00 per share this year, and approximate $38.00 per share in 2027.
  • vyvgart looks well-entrenched as the best solution for myasthenia gravis (mg).
    although competition exists, primarily from astrazeneca, argenx's solution appears superior for now from both efficacy and safety perspectives. u.s. manufacturing capabilities are being expanded, since pipeline growth is reportedly positive (see below).
  • the market position was enhanced recently by a new pre-filled syringe product.
    management contends that the market will rise to 60,000 addressable patients by 2030, from 17,000, thanks to seronegative (an fda decision is expected by year-end), ocular, and biologics markets within the overall mg market. Five registrational readouts are scheduled for this year.
  • investors may gain a greater sense of the potential to penetrate new markets.
    registrational readouts for multifocal motor neuropathy, thyroid eye disease, idiopathic inflammatory myopathy, and immune thrombocytopenia will likely be conducted. this neutrally ranked stock is suitable for aggressive growth investors that are able to tolerate risk. granted, this high-flying biotechnology equity has a perfect growth consistency score and an average risk rank. however, future prospects are not well defined, and neutrally-ranked argenx stock carries a lofty p/e multiple.
