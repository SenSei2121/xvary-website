What they do
EVI sells, installs, and repairs commercial laundry equipment for businesses that run big washers and dryers.
EVI is a value-added distributor, which means it sells the machine, then charges for the work around it. So you get planning, design, consulting, install, maintenance, and repair in one bill. That is harder to replace than a plain equipment sale, especially with 850 employees on the ground.
