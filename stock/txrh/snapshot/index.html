the number that mattered
Restaurant margin dollars in Q4— commodity and wage inflation vs. traffic— decide whether the FY compounder narrative still holds at ~29x trailing earnings.
  • texas roadhouse’s 2025 fourth-quarter earnings were likely down from a year ago.
  • the company was poised to announce its december-period results as we went to press.
  • share earnings likely declined about 10% from 2024’s tally driven by a high mid-single-digit increase in food cost inflation.
  • we are looking for earnings per share of between $1.50 and $1.60, versus 2024’s $1.73.
    note, that the company is also lapping a 14week fourth quarter last year versus the more traditional 13-week period they are facing this year.
  • although the company took a 1.7% price increase at the beginning of the quarter, we don’t think it will be enough to offset both the lost week, and the higher food costs.
    we think the operating margin will contract about 160 basis points in the fourth period due to the foodcost inflation and higher labor expenses.
