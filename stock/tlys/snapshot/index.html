What they do
Tilly's sells casual clothes, shoes, accessories, and skate-surf gear through 230 stores and its website.
Tilly's wins when your shopping trip is about a full vibe, not one item. It sells apparel, shoes, accessories, and hard-goods in one place across 230 stores in 33 states, plus tillys.com. That mix gives you one basket for hoodies, Vans, sunglasses, and a skateboard, which is harder to copy than a single-product store.
