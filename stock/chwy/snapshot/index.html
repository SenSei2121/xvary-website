What just happened
beat estimates
Chewy beat by 6.67% as revenue hit $3.12B and gross margin held at 29.8%.
Sales rose 8%, and autoship grew 14% to $2.61B. That mix is why the quarter looked cleaner than the stock's 26.0x multiple.

autoship sales
Autoship hit $2.61B, and 14% growth there matters because repeat orders are the whole trick.
  • the pet-product retailer reported third-quarter sales of $3.12 billion, reflecting an 8% vs. prior year increase.
    this growth was primarily driven by unit volume rather than price increases, highlighting the strength of chewy’s customer base and operational model.
  • chewy’s autoship program continued to be a standout performer, with sales growing 14%, to $2.61 billion.
    autoship revenues, which are highly predictable, provide operational advantages by reducing costs and improving margins.
  • profitability was another highlight, with gross margins expanding by 50 basis points to 29.8%, driven by sponsored ad growth, a strong autoship baseline, and favorable category mix.
    free cash flow generation was also impressive, reaching $176 million, up nearly $70 million sequentially. this allowed chewy to repurchase $55 million worth of shares, while continuing to invest in strategic initiatives.
  • the florida-based company continues to make strides in its health offerings.
    we’re encouraged by the expansion of chewy vet care (cvc) practices and the pending acquisition (slated to close by february 2026) of smartequine, a premium equine health brand. these initiatives are expected to enhance customer loyalty, expand market share, and improve margins. additionally, chewy+, the company’s paid membership program, has been driving higher order frequency, broader category engagement, and stronger autoship participation. despite raising the annual fee from $49 to $79, the program has maintained strong conversion rates and is no longer dilutive to margins.
  • chewy appears well-positioned for future growth.

What could go wrong

the top threat is trade-down and promotion pressure in chewy's core pet basket.
