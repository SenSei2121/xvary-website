how to read it
GAAP net income fell YoY on investment and benefit dynamics; adjusted operating EPS held at $1.92—the metric management emphasizes for segment trends.
  • unum group continues to manage against higher costs.
  • although the quarterly income (i.e., revenue) advance accelerated to about 5% vs. prior year in the september interim, benefits payments rose even faster.
    note that the third quarter is typically when the life insurer updates its annual reserve assumptions for gaap purposes. this year's re-measurements, which swung to a large pre-tax loss from a gain in 2024, were most impacted by experience updates for unum's long-term care unit.
  • and, even though we eliminate much of these non-cash accruals from our results presentation, the adjusted benefits ratio still rose around 200 basis points from last year's level, to 86.6%.
  • thus, comparative earnings per share of $2.09 declined modestly.
  • although the figure matched our september call, it trailed the street's estimate for the third-consecutive quarter.
