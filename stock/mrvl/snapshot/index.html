Revenue was $2.2B. That is the number that shows how much scale the business now has.
  • marvell technology closed fiscal 2026 (year ended january 31st) on a strong note.
  • fiscal fourth-quarter revenue of $2.2 billion rose 22% from the prior year, and full-year revenue reached $8.2 billion, up 42%.
  • data center demand drove the quarterly result, with that segment (74% of total revenue) posting 21% vs. prior year growth in the january period.
    adjusted earnings of $0.80 per share came in above guidance, as the operating margin is benefiting from higher-value optical and custom products, which are growing their share of the mix.
  • the established product lines are performing well above prior expectations.
  • the interconnect business, which includes high-speed pam digital signal processors and coherent data center interconnect modules, is now expected to grow more than 50% in fiscal 2026, well above the 30% management had projected just three months ago.
    the data center switching segment should surpass $600 million in fiscal 2026 revenue, up from a $500 million target issued last quarter, with the next-generation 51.2-terabit platform ramping alongside continued demand for current products.
