What they do
Codexis makes enzymes that help drug makers build medicines with fewer chemical steps.
CodeEvolver is directed evolution, which means lab-guided trial and error. That matters because your customers pay for performance, not a catalog. A single Merck technology transfer brought in $37.8M, or 53.6% of FY25 revenue, so Codexis sells know-how, not widgets.

What could go wrong

the #1 risk is Merck concentration inside a still-small revenue base.

Merck concentration
A single $37.8M Merck technology transfer did a lot of the work in the latest quarter. For a company with $70.4M in full-year 2025 revenue, that is not background noise — that is the story.
If large partner work fades without replacement, the growth case breaks fast.

high
Cash runway and dilution risk
Management says cash lasts through 2027, while analysts still model a 2026 net loss of $36.85M. That means time is finite and profitability is still out on the horizon.
If burn stays high into 2027, a capital raise becomes the obvious outcome.
