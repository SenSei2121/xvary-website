What they do
Innovex makes and rents critical oilfield parts that help customers build, finish, and keep wells producing.
When a well part fails, your customer does not lose patience. They lose rig time and cash. Innovex sells into that pain across the full well lifecycle, and 55% of revenue comes from the U.S. and Canadian onshore market, where speed and reliability decide who gets the next order. Low debt helps too: long-term debt is just $63 million, or 4% of capital.
