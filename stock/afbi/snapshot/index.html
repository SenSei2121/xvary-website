What they do
It takes deposits and makes loans for Georgia businesses and households.
Affinity has 3 full-service offices and 1 loan production office in Georgia. That is a small footprint, but it keeps the bank close to local borrowers. It has 88 employees, so your money is handled by a small team, not a call center maze.
