Flagstar takes deposits and makes loans, mostly to apartment owners and businesses across the Northeast and Midwest.

EPS vs estimate
The $0.06 EPS matters because it is the first sign the bank is climbing out of the loss pile, even with EDGAR still showing a broader loss.
  • flagstar bank eliminated its status as a holding company.
    the move, finalized in october, reduces regulation, helps streamline administrative functions and creates $15 million in annual cost savings.
  • the move changed the bank's name from flagstar financial to flagstar bank, n.a.
  • flagstar appears to be succeeding in digging itself out of a hole.
    in early 2024, the former new york community bancorp gave the FDIC the impression that it had strong management and finances, and was thus permitted to purchase the failed signature bank.
  • the news quickly lifted nycb shares to multi-year highs.
    there is no legal decision, but it seems widely accepted that the bank's accounting practices were worse than questionable.
  • news of the errors sent the shares plummeting 85%.
    the former management and board were largely removed, and the government appears to have insured future compliance by appointing its choice of a new president and ceo.
