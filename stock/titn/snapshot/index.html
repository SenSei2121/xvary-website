What they do
Titan Machinery sells and services farm and construction equipment in the U.S. and Europe.
Titan's edge is the same thing that annoys customers: a full-service dealer network. You buy the machine, then the parts, then the repair, so leaving means rebuilding every relationship. It has 3,340 employees and 3 segments, which gives the business more reach than a one-lot dealer.
