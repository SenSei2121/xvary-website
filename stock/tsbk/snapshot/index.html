What they do
It takes local deposits and turns them into home, construction, and commercial loans across 24 Washington branches.
This business wins the old-fashioned way: your banker is down the road, not inside an app. Timberland runs 24 branches across six Washington counties, and that local presence helps it keep relationships sticky when you need a mortgage, construction loan, or a place to park cash. The balance sheet is not trying anything cute either: long-term debt is just $20 million, or 6% of capital, which means fewer ways for your boring bank to surprise you.

What could go wrong

the #1 risk is washington commercial real estate concentration. TSBK is a local lender, which sounds comforting until the local cycle turns against you.

∘
med
One-state exposure
100% of the $2.1B loan book is tied to Washington. There is no geographic release valve if the local economy slows.
That makes your diversification problem obvious: one state carries the credit book, the deposit base, and the operating footprint.
∘
med
Commercial real estate sensitivity
Commercial real estate loans total $612M, and multi-family adds another $212M. Put those side by side and you get a bank that is heavily tied to local property demand.
If occupancy, refinancing, or valuations weaken, cheap on earnings stops feeling cheap.
∘
med
Margin compression
Net interest income is $31.3M. Non-interest income is $2.1M. When your fee income is that small, funding costs have a direct line into earnings.
If deposit costs rise faster than loan yields, the income statement tightens fast.
∘
med
Leadership has to keep the bank boring
The prior CEO retired in 2023. That does not make this a crisis. It does mean the current team has to protect an 11.73% return on equity without stretching for growth.
For community banks, the mistake is usually not dramatic. It is a few bad loans made when everyone felt smart.
Put it together and the risk picture is clear: a $299M stock backed by a $2.1B Washington loan book works well when credit stays clean. If that local property exposure cracks, the multiple will not save you.
