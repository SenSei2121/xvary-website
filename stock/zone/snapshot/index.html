What they do
CleanCore sells ozone-based cleaning machines and tablets that let you clean with generated ozone instead of traditional chemicals.
The pitch is simple: make cleaning chemicals on site with water and ozone, then skip buying and storing jugs. If that works for your facility, the refill habit can stick because the machine is already on your wall or cart. The problem is scale: annual revenue is only $2 million, so the product idea exists before the moat does.
