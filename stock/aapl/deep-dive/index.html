The M5 chip family, launched in October 2025 across MacBook Pro, iPad Pro, and Vision Pro, emphasizes performance-per-watt advantages and Neural Engine enhancements for on-device AI processing, targeting over 95% on-device execution for privacy-focused Apple Intelligence features. This contrasts with commodity Android supply chains and cloud-heavy competitor approaches...

Key near-term deliverables include additional M5 variants for Mac mini and Mac Studio in early 2026, potential OLED iPad refreshes, and continued iPhone 18 preparations (rumored foldable/under-display elements in 2026-2027).
