What they do
OppFi uses bank partners and software to make loans to Americans mainstream lenders usually reject.
Big banks reject a lot of people. OppFi is built around the 48 million Americans who face credit insecurity. If your credit file gets you shrugged off elsewhere, speed and approval matter more than branding, and that gives OppFi room to charge for access.
