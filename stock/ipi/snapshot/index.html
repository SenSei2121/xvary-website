What they do
IPI digs and sells potash, Trio fertilizer, water, salt, and other mineral products for farms and oilfield customers.
You get a rare U.S. supply chain. Intrepid is the only domestic producer of muriate of potash, a potassium fertilizer, and it runs 3 solar facilities plus 1 underground Trio mine. Your crops, feed, and oilfield orders depend on local delivery, and local delivery beats long-haul freight when the product is heavy.
