What they do
Nature’s Sunshine makes and sells supplements and personal care products through a direct-selling network in more than 40 countries.
This business wins by owning the product and the sales channel at the same time. Direct selling → independent distributors selling person-to-person → so what: you do not need expensive store shelves to reach customers in more than 40 countries. The cushion is real but not huge: gross margin was 72.4%, while long-term debt was just $10 million, or 2% of capital, so you get room to absorb mistakes without blowing up.
