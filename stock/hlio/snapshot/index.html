What they do
Helios sells the hydraulic and electronic parts that help industrial machines move, connect, and show operators what the system is doing.
Helios wins by getting designed into equipment before the machine is built. Design-in (picked at the engineering stage → hard to replace later → your customer is sticky) matters because Hydraulics produced 65% of 2024 revenue, according to the company description. If your machine already uses Helios valves, couplings, and controls, swapping them out risks downtime, redesign costs, and testing delays.
