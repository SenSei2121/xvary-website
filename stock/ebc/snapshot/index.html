What they do
Eastern Bankshares is a regional bank that sells checking, loans, and wealth management across New England.
Eastern has 110 branches across eastern Massachusetts, southern and coastal New Hampshire, Rhode Island, and Connecticut, according to the company profile. It also owns Cambridge Trust Wealth Management, which oversees about $9.2 billion in assets. If your checking account, loan, and adviser all sit in one place, moving your money gets annoying fast.

The #1 risk is HarborOne integration execution.

Merger integration execution
HarborOne closed on Nov. 1, 2025. If the combined bank cannot hold near the recent 1.36% return on assets, the market will stop treating this as a cleanup story and start treating it as a bad deal.
Impact: the premium multiple has less cover if profitability slips.

med
Valuation compression
80.7x trailing earnings is not a normal bank multiple. If earnings do not rise fast enough, the stock does not need bad news to fall. It only needs the market to demand a more normal price.
Impact: multiple compression can hit even if the bank stays profitable.

Low earnings predictability
A 10/100 earnings predictability score is the market's way of saying the quarter may not read cleanly. That matters more after a merger, when investors are already trying to separate one-time noise from the real run rate.
