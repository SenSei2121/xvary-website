What they do
Clearwater Paper makes the paperboard and tissue products behind cartons, cups, paper towels, and toilet paper.
You keep buying paper goods even when your budget is tight. Clearwater sells private-label tissue and packaging inputs that retailers and converters need every week. That is why revenue still hit $1.6B, and still sees $2.0B in 2026 sales, even after FY2024 EPS fell to -$4.41.
