What they do
BBSI handles payroll, benefits, workers' comp, and HR so small businesses can avoid expensive administrative mistakes.
BBSI wins by bundling the boring stuff you cannot screw up. Payroll, benefits, workers' comp, safety, and HR admin come in one system, delivered by local teams usually within 50 miles of clients across 47 states and D.C. That creates switching costs (switching costs → leaving is painful and risky → clients think twice before moving) and helps produce a 23.9% return on capital.
