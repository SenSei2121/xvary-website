the number that mattered
The number that matters is $0.25 in full-year 2025 EPS, because 108.8x trailing earnings leaves almost no room for another weak year.
  • at the time of the announcement the deal was worth $30 per share and was a 41% premium to the prior closing price of golden entertainment's stock price.
    recently, vici's stock has fallen slightly to $27.60, implying a total transaction value of $27.65 per share.
  • although business has been challenging for golden in the past year with lower visitation to las vegas, free cash flow was still healthy and despite the premium, the deal does not seem like a particularly robust price for shareholders.
    that said, a `go-shop` period was provided through early december and no other offers were forthcoming.
  • the transactions are expected to be completed by mid-2026 after customary regulatory approvals.
  • recent results have been lackluster.
  • most of golden's casinos cater to nevada locals and depend on the regional economy.
    also, its main property on the strip, the strat, generally benefits from overflow demand, which rarely exists in the current economic climate.
