Investment Thesis

This basket targets the infrastructure backbone powering the artificial intelligence revolution through 17 names spanning real estate investment trusts, bitcoin miners pivoting to high-performance computing facilities, and technology hardware providers. It captures ownership and development of data center real estate, power-secured sites for hyperscale tenants, and the servers and systems filling those facilities. The investment logic rests on explosive AI-driven demand: hyperscalers plan hundreds of billions in annual capital expenditures, with global data center capex projected to approach $1 trillion in 2026 and reach $1.7 trillion cumulatively by 2030, fueled by training and inference workloads requiring massive power capacity and low-latency infrastructure. Prologis (PLD) at 9.3% weight commits up to $25-50 billion toward data center development on its land bank, while Cipher Digital (CIFR) at equal weight pivots its 4+ GW pipeline to long-term HPC leases with AWS, Google-linked partners, and others, generating contracted revenue visibility. Dell Technologies (DELL) at 9.2% leads the server market with AI-optimized systems, guiding AI server revenue toward $50 billion in its fiscal 2027 on strong order backlogs exceeding $60 billion. The portfolio's effective N of 14.1 ensures diversified yet concentrated exposure across these interdependent layers of the ecosystem.

Key risks include power and grid constraints that could delay builds or inflate costs, escalating development expenses now rising at a 6-7% CAGR to over $11 million per MW, potential oversupply in secondary markets from speculative construction, and technological obsolescence if AI model efficiencies reduce compute intensity faster than anticipated. Interest rate sensitivity remains elevated for REIT structures and capital-intensive developers, while tenant concentration on a handful of hyperscalers introduces execution and renewal risks on multi-year leases. The highest-conviction position is Dell Technologies, which commands leading market share in accelerated servers and benefits directly from hyperscaler deployments without the heavy balance sheet leverage or development risks inherent to pure real estate plays, positioning it for outsized revenue growth as AI infrastructure scales.
